Egusi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups egusi (melon seeds), ground
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups assorted meat (beef, tripe, etc.), cooked
  • 1 cup stockfish, soaked and shredded
  • 1 cup dried fish, cleaned and deboned
  • 2 cups waterleaf or spinach, chopped
  • 2 cups ugu (fluted pumpkin leaves), chopped
  • 2-3 Scotch bonnet peppers, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons crayfish, ground
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the palm oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add the ground egusi to the pot and stir continuously to prevent lumps. Cook for about 10 minutes until the egusi is well cooked and fragrant.
  3. Add the assorted meat, stockfish, and dried fish to the pot. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Add the chopped Scotch bonnet peppers and ground crayfish. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Add a little water if the mixture is too thick. Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the chopped waterleaf or spinach and ugu leaves to the pot. Stir well and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot with pounded yam, fufu, or any preferred side dish.
