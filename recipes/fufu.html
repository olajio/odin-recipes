Fufu

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cassava flour or yam flour
  • 4 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water: In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add the flour: Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to medium and gradually add the cassava or yam flour while stirring continuously with a wooden spoon or spatula to prevent lumps from forming.
  3. Cook the fufu: Continue to stir the mixture vigorously as it thickens. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the fufu is smooth, thick, and stretchy. If it becomes too thick, you can add a little more water to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Shape and serve: Once cooked, scoop the fufu onto a plate and shape it into a mound using the back of a spoon. Serve hot with your favorite soup or stew, such as egusi, ogbono, or vegetable soup.
